How it all started:

In our school, in grade 4, I started learning about renewable energy. During Diwali (an Indian festival known as the festival of lights mostly celebrated by hindus), we had to create a startup business idea and present it to some judges. This experience, made me realise that those of even ten years old can make and run businesses. That's when I decided to make Prime Solar. Prime Solar is a business that produces and distributes solar panels in India, and potentially with enough growth, we could go overseas. Now, this business is still in its planning phases.
